ANSWER:

User Scenarios:
1. As a player, I can roll the dice to move my token around the board.
2. As a player, I can buy properties when I land on them if they are not already owned.
3. As a player, I can pay rent to the owner of a property when I land on it.
4. As a player, I can draw a Chance or Community Chest card when I land on the respective spaces.

Acceptance Tests:
1. GIVEN it is my turn,
   WHEN I roll the dice,
   THEN my token should move forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.

2. GIVEN I land on an unowned property,
   WHEN I choose to buy the property,
   THEN the property should be marked as owned by me,
   AND the cost of the property should be deducted from my balance.

3. GIVEN I land on a property owned by another player,
   WHEN I land on the property,
   THEN I should pay the rent amount to the owner,
   AND the rent amount should be deducted from my balance,
   AND the rent amount should be added to the owner's balance.

4. GIVEN I land on a Chance or Community Chest space,
   WHEN I draw a card,
   THEN the card's instructions should be executed,
   AND any changes to my balance or position should be applied as per the card's instructions.
