PARO Nokia 2024/2025 - Workshop Participation
About the Course

Practical Aspects of Software Development (PARO) is a series of 15 classes conducted at the University of Wrocław and Wrocław University of Science and Technology, delivered by Nokia employees.
My Participation

I participated in the PARO Nokia workshops during the 2024/2025 academic year, gaining hands-on experience in modern software development practices with emphasis on C++ and agile methodologies.

    Code optimization techniques

    Building complex IT systems

Learning Outcomes
